Advancing

knowledge and education


The mission of the Smart Infrastructure Lab (SIL) is to advance knowledge and education in the areas of sustainable infrastructure development, digital transformation, and smart infrastructure systems.



        The center supports interdisciplinary research and education programs that integrate science and engineering theories and construction practices in the areas of trenchless technologies, asset management, construction technologies, digital visualization, IoTs and Sensing, virtual construction service platform, and advanced decision-making systems, machine learning and artificial intelligence.
